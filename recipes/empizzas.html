Fast English Muffin Pizzas

Ingredients

  • 4 split English muffins
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 16 slices pepperoni sausage (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 (fahrenheit) or 190 (celsius).
  2. Place the English muffin halves cut side up onto a baking sheet, spoon pizza sauce onto each one. Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni (if added).
  3. Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the cheese is melted and browned on the edges.
