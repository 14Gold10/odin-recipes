Easy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder. Make a well in the centre and pour in the milk, egg, and oil. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/3 cup for each pancakel Brown on both sides and serve hot.
