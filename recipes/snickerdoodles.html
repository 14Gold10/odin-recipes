Snickerdoodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • Snickerdoodles:
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Coating:
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

Snickerdoodles:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and baking powder
  2. In the bowl of your mixer, beat the butter and sugar until smooth (around 2min 30sec). Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Scrape down the sides of the bowl. Beat in the vanilla extract. Add the flour mixture and beat until you have a smooth dough. If the dough is soft, cover and refrigerate until firm enough to roll into balls (around 1hr 30min).
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 (fahrenheit) or 190 (celsius) and place rack in the centre of the oven. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Shape the dough into 1 inch round balls.

Coating:

  1. In a large shallow bowl, mix together the sugar and cinnamon.
  2. Roll the balls of douugh in the cinnamon sugar and place on the prepared pan, spacing around 2 inches apart. Then, using the bottom of a glass, gently flatten each cookie to about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Bake the cookies for around 9 minutes or until they are light golden brown around the edges. Place on a wire rack to cool.
  4. Makes about 72 cookies
